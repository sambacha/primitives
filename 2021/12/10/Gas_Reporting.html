Fee Speed Definitions

  • Fastest: next block (i.e. <30 seconds)
  • Fast: below 2 minutes (<10 blocks)
  • Medium: around 5 minutes (<20 blocks)
  • Slow: below 30 minutes (a.k.a safe-low, <120 blocks)
